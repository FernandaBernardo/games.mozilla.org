About GDC

Connect with Mozilla engineers and other leaders for a three-day exploration of building beautiful and performant games on the Web.

Partners

Demos

  • Teleporter

    A demo showing the next generation of 3D on the web: physical-based shading, fully deferred rendering, and global illumination.

    • Partner: Unity
    • Technologies: WebGL2

  • Chase

    Showcasing the sky's-the-limit potential of the Unity game engine for bringing exceptional visual fidelity and performance to the web on handheld devices.

    • Partner: Unity
    • Technologies: WebGL

  • Epic Unreal Engine with WebVR

    A preview of how the Web is evolving as a powerful platform for virtual reality.

    • Partner: Epic
    • Technologies: WebGL2, WebVR

  • Oculus Rex

    WebGL and the Oculus Rift work together to provide a fun and thrilling experience.

    Developed in C++11 with the Minko open-source 3D engine and powered by Mozilla's asm.js and Emscripten technologies.

    • Partner: Aerys
    • Technologies: WebGL, asm.js, emscripten

  • Tanx

    A completely cross-platform, tank battle MMO game.

    • Partner: PlayCanvas
    • Technologies: WebGL, WebRTC, WebSockets, Gamepad API, Canvas

  • The Marvellous Miss Take

    In full WebGL glory, the stealth genre gets turned on its head in this heist adventure.

    • Partner: Turbulenz
    • Technologies: WebGL (handwritten), WebSockets, Gamepad API
